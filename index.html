Welcome to Marked Souls

About Marked Souls

Marked Souls is a North American based online gaming community which was originally created in 2007 as a Guild Wars guild. Marked Souls is primarily a PvE guild. You can expect guild missions, events, raids, etc.

Who makes an ideal Marked Souls member?

An ideal recruit has a good sense of humor, enjoys playing with other guild members, and strives to become a better player. In Marked Souls, participation in the community counts for a lot more than your current skill at the game. We have experienced players willing to work with players that feels they need help in a specific area (game mode, class, etc.).

Requirements:

  • Be respectful
  • Speak English
  • Pass a probation period, during which the player must show that they can fit in with the guild’s expectations and current members.
What does Marked Souls offer?
  • Great friendly people and a group of players that understand the value of support and encouragement.
  • Experience and help! Members range from veteran players to enthusiastic newcomers and all in between.
  • A mature community with members ranging from casual to semi-hardcore members.
  • Discord server for voice and text chat. Members are online most of the day whether in game or not.
  • No required activities – No donations of faction, items, or time are ever required.
  • Representation Policy – Represent Marked Souls when you’re playing with Marked Souls.
